Quick setup

Install in three steps

No terminal commands.
No account required.

  1. 01

    Download

    Get the ZIP for your operating system and extract it.

  2. 02

    Run the installer

    Open the included graphical installer and select your VPX and tables folders.

  3. 03

    Play

    Finish a supported PinMAME game. Your score is recorded automatically.
